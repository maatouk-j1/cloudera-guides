## Notes on these references

The first two entries are pinned to `latest` rather than to a release, so they track whatever Cloudera currently publishes rather than the 7.3.2 / Data Services 1.5.5 SP2 build this guide installs. The three product overviews are pinned to **1.5.5**, matching the Data Services version in the guide's own version table.

Three further references — the release notes, the Base requirements and supported versions, and the ECS installation requirements — sit at the end of [Glossary of Acronyms](/installations/cdp-7-3-2/appendix/glossary-acronyms) in the source rather than here.
